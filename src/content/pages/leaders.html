How MVP Score is Calculated

At Rise Up League, we currently track PTS, REB, AST, STL, and BLK stats. Efficiency stats (FGM/FGA, 3PM/3PA, FTM/FTA) are not yet included in the formula.

Requirements & Formula

1

Player must have played in at least 5 regular season games

2

Sum of the following weighted stats:

  • PPG× 3.0
  • RPG× 2.0
  • APG× 2.0
  • SPG× 2.0
  • BPG× 2.0
  • Win %× 3.0

Example Calculation

A player averaging 22.4 PPG, 10.2 RPG, 3.4 APG, 0.8 SPG, 0.2 BPG — Team record: 6-1 (85.7% win%)

22.4 PPG × 3.0= 67.2
10.2 RPG × 2.0= 20.4
3.4 APG × 2.0= 6.8
0.8 SPG × 2.0= 1.6
0.2 BPG × 2.0= 0.4
0.857 Win% × 3.0= 2.57
Final MVP Score 98.97
